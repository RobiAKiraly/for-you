If you’re reading this, it means you found your way here, to this small, quiet place I built with you in mind.

I wanted to write down what I don’t always say out loud: that you make ordinary days feel like something worth remembering. That the way you laugh at your own jokes before you finish telling them is one of my favorite sounds in the world. That I fall a little more in love every time you forget I’m watching you.

You are the calm at the end of my longest days and the first person I want to tell when something good happens. Loving you doesn’t feel like work, it feels like coming home.

So here is my promise, written in pixels instead of ink, but meant with everything in me: I will keep choosing you. Today, tomorrow, and every ordinary, extraordinary day after that.

Always yours ❤️
